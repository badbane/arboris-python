arboris-python by sbarthelemy

a simulator for tree-shaped interactive robotic systems.

arboris-python is a simulator for mechanical systems written in python. It follows a [matlab version](https://vizir.robot.jussieu.fr/trac/arboris), now (almost) discontinued.

Dependencies

- numpy - h5py (optional) - osgswig (optional) - cvxmod (optional)
